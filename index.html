artwork
Having taken art classes as early as first grade, I have always believed that art is about expression and emotion. I came to love illustrating details via pen in particular, and it quickly became my speciality. I am proud to have established a signature art style that I am pleased with. I use a Uni pin fine line pigment ink pen and Pentel Stylo sketch pen on 120GSM paper.

web development
My first experience with web development began on the blogging site Tumblr, a platform in which users can choose a blog theme, or create and upload their own. I couldn't find a theme with features that catered directly to my preferences, and so I decided to make my own by learning how to code together with Tumblr's API. In summer 2015, I established glenthemes, a blog to share my themes for public use. As of January 2019, my free themes have 270k+ hits on Pastebin, and my premium themes have served over 60 customers in the past year.
