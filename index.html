    artwork — info etc.
    Having drawn all my life, it wasn't until my penultimate year of high school that I fell in love with ink pen drawings. When asked "what happens if you make a mistake?", my answer is simple: there are no mistakes when it comes to art; they are all part of the piece. Developing such an art style as my speciality has molded me into a meticulous perfectionist, and I take pride with every stroke that I cast on paper.

    Aside from traditional art, I also produce digital colourings of my art. I have been acquainted with Photoshop for 5 years.

    style & branding —
    I enjoy crafting layouts that are intuitive and straightforward, but futuristic and elaborate at the same time. My designs aim to be rich with an elevated aesthetic without complicating the user's experience.

    If I was stranded on an island and I could only choose one line of code to survive on, it would be display:flex;. In design, spacing and colour choice are aspects that I highly value.

    #ht > .branding {
    display:flex;
    font-family:Playfair Display;
    background-color:#fcfcfc;
